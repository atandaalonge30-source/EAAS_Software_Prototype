Dual-Factor Biometric Engine Online

Access decisions that read
identity and emotion.

A machine-learning authentication prototype that pairs facial identity verification (LBPH) with real-time emotion-state analysis (ANN classifier) to flag coerced, spoofed or abnormal login attempts that password and fingerprint systems miss.

Enrol a New User Authenticate / Scan
1

Facial Identification

Haar-cascade face localisation feeds a Local Binary Pattern Histogram (LBPH) recognizer trained per-user during enrolment, returning an identity-similarity score.

2

Emotion Recognition

Region-based facial feature extraction (brow / eye / mouth) is classified by a scikit-learn artificial neural network into Neutral, Happy, Sad, Angry or Surprised.

3

Fusion Decision Engine

Identity and emotion scores are combined by a weighted decision unit that grants, denies, or escalates a login to additional verification.
